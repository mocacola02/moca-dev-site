Snape's Dungeon Dark Ride

Ride on a fun dark ride and collect as many beans as possible!

This map was developed in 15 days for the Halloween 2025 HP Mapping Contest, where it won first place!

Now improved with bug fixes and rebalanced scoring!

Rank Requirements

  • Rank S: 1000+ Beans
  • Rank A: 700 - 999 Beans
  • Rank B: 525 - 699 Beans
  • Rank C: 400 - 524 Beans
  • Rank D: 200 - 399 Beans
  • Rank F: Below 200 Beans
