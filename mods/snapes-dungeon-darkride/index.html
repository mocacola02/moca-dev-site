Snape's Dungeon Dark Ride

Collect as many beans as possible in this fun dark ride!

Rank S: 1000+ Beans
Rank A: 700 - 999 Beans
Rank B: 525 - 699 Beans
Rank C: 400 - 524 Beans
Rank D: 200 - 399 Beans
Rank F: Below 200

This map was developed within 15 days for the Halloween 2025 HP Mapping Contest, where it won first place!
